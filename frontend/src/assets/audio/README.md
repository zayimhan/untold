## Requirements

- All tracks: CC0 or equivalent license (free for any use)
- Ambient loops: 30–60s, loop-safe (no obvious seam), no vocals, no distinct melody
- Feel: old — a little tape hiss or wobble is intentional, not a defect
- knock.mp3: warm, not sharp — a fist on a thick wooden door, with room reverb

## Filter on Freesound.org

1. Search the keyword above
2. Filter by **License: Creative Commons 0**
3. Preview — listen for mood, not just the right sound
4. Download MP3
